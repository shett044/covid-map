Coronavirus Case Map Marker

 

Most important Read Me:

 

This map is crowdsourced map which aims at displaying coronavirus affected localities/buildings on google map.

Every individual can add/ report confirmed coronavirus cases in their locality.

This would help people know local coronavirus hotspot near them and they would be able to avoid them.

 

Steps to Mark Coronavirus Confirm case in your locality

1) Search by area/building name in your locality

2) Click on building / locality in the map.

3) A yellow Marker will appear with "Report Case" button.

4) Click on "Report Case" to add confirmed coronavirus case location.

5) If you do mistake while Marking, Click on same marker and pop will appear

"Do you want to delete awaiting marker?" . Click on marker to deselect current location

 and proceed with marking new location
